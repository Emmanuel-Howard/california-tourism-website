Capital of California


Sacramento, the vibrant capital of California, is a city rich in history, culture, and economic significance. Known as the birthplace of the Gold Rush, it has evolved into a thriving metropolitan area with a unique blend of historic landmarks, modern infrastructure, and a diverse community. Home to the iconic California State Capitol, scenic riverfronts, and a growing arts and food scene, Sacramento offers residents and visitors a dynamic mix of urban energy and natural beauty. Its central location makes it a key political and economic hub, attracting businesses, government institutions, and entrepreneurs looking to make an impact in the Golden State.
